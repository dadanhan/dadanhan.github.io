About Me

I am an Applied Mathematics PhD student with a background in Theoretical Physics currently applying myself to problems in Biology. Originally from Sydney, Australia, I moved to Manchester and completed my combined Bachelors and Masters in Physics with Theoretical Physics. Funded by the University of Manchester and the Wellcome Trust as of 2017, I am pursuing a PhD in Applied Mathematics with a focus on modelling intracellular processes.
